Description: 
A directed, weighted network representing the neural network of C. Elegans. Data compiled by D. Watts and S. Strogatz and made available on at http://www-personal.umich.edu/~mejn/netdata/celegansneural.zip. Original experimental data taken from J. G. White, E. Southgate, J. N. Thompson, and S. Brenner, Phil. Trans. R. Soc. London 314, 1-340 (1986).

Graph Info:
Nodes: 297
Edges: 2359
Unique Weights: 31
Directed

Referrences:
  J. G. White, E. Southgate, J. N. Thompson, and S. Brenner, "The structure
  of the nervous system of the nematode C. Elegans", Phil. Trans. R. Soc.
  London 314, 1-340 (1986).

  D. J. Watts and S. H. Strogatz, "Collective dynamics of `small-world'
  networks", Nature 393, 440-442 (1998).
